# Task

Read `vendor_notes.txt` in this directory. It contains raw notes comparing
three vendors for a hypothetical contract. In your final reply (do not write
any file), write a plain-text recommendation brief, under 100 words, that:
- names the recommended vendor
- states the one clear reason driving the recommendation
- names at least one risk or tradeoff of that choice

Do not just list all three vendors neutrally; make and justify a pick.
